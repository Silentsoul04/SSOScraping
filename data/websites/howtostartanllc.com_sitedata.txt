                       </div>\n\n                        <div class=\"col-md-10\">\n                            <p style=\"margin-top:20px;\"><strong>A registered agent is absolutely required to form This ensures that they can accept important legal documents, taking the pressure off of you to always be available.</li>\n                                <li> Having a registered agent service allows you to form an LLC in any state you do not reside in. A registered agents address is a public record. Choosing a registered agent service ensures your privacy.</li>\n                                <li>On a related note, you can use the office address of the registered agent service as your business headquarters.</li>\n                                <li>In the unfortunate case you are sued, lawsuits will be served to the address of your registered agent rather than your place of business in front of your employees.</li>\n                            </ul>\n                        </div>\n\n                        <div class=\"col-sm-2\">\n                            <img class=\"img-responsive hidden-xs hidden-sm pull-left\" style=\"padding:10px;\" src=\"/web/images/learn-the-basics/what-is-a-registered-agent-page/compliance-icon.png\" alt=\"A Registered Agent Service provides privacy\">\n                        </div>\n\n                        <div class=\"col-sm-10\">\n\n                            <h3>You will be in compliance</h3>\n                            <ul class=\"padded-list\" style=\"max-width:700px;\">\n                                <li>You have the option to choose a service that will remind you of any State filings that are due and make sure you stay in compliance.</li>\n                                <li>The registered agent service can assist you in finding state-specific accountants and legal professionals.</li>\n                                <li>The registered agent service can keep copies of your corporate documents in case of theft, loss, or natural disaster.</li>\n                            </ul>\n
