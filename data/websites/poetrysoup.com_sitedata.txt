I touched\n her doll skin. It felt not of porcelain, but cold paper. I smiled at her shadow. \nI envied her blank, lifeless eyes.\n\n</pre>\n<br>\n<p>\nCopyright © <a href=\"/poems_poets/poems_by_poet.aspx?ID=84949\">Glennerd Williams</a> | Year Posted
