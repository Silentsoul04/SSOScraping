represents the family of ARM SoCs from <a href=\"/Allwinner\" title=\"Allwinner\">Allwinner Technology</a>, a Chinese fabless semiconductor company. Their best known products are the <b>sunxi</b> SoC series, such as the A10 (sun4i), A13 (sun5i) and A20 (sun7i) chips, which were very successful in the low-budget tablet market. See <a href=\"/Allwinner_SoC_Family\" title=\"Allwinner SoC Family\">Allwinner SoC Family</a> for more information on the different Allwinner chips.\n</p>\n<h1> <span class=\"mw-headline\" id=\"Community\"> Community </span></h1>\n<p>The <a href=\"/Category:Community\" title=\"Category:Community\">sunxi community</a> is a very active and advanced one. For more information, check our <a href=\"/Category:Community\" title=\"Category:Community\">linux-sunxi community page</a>. It explains how to get help and how to contact us, and how you can contribute as well.\n</p><p><b><a href=\"/Allwinner\" title=\"Allwinner\">Allwinner</a> does not actively participate in or support this community. In fact, it is <a href=\"/GPL_Violations\" title=\"GPL Violations\">violating the GPLv2 license</a> in several ways and has so far not shown willingness to resolve this.</b>\n</p>\n<h1> <span class=\"mw-headline\" id=\"Allwinner_based_devices\"> Allwinner based devices </span></h1>\n<p>Due to the popularity of the cheap Allwinner SoCs, there are literally thousands of different devices out there.\n</p><p>If you are looking for support for your device from the <a href=\"/Category:Community\" title=\"Category:Community\">sunxi community</a> here, please take a look at our <a href=\"/Identification_guide\" title=\"Identification guide\"> guide to identifying your device</a>. If you cannot find your exact device, then you need to work through our <a href=\"/New_Device_howto\" title=\"New Device howto\"> new device howto</a> to get it supported.\n</p><p>If you are interested in buying an Allwinner SoC based device, then read through our <a href=\"/Buying_guide\" title=\"Buying guide\"> buying guide</a>.\n</p>\n<h1> <span class=\"mw-headline\"
