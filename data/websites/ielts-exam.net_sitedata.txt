International English Language \n\t\t\t\t\t\tTesting System, is designed to assess the language ability \n\t\t\t\t\t\tof candidates who need to study or work where English is \n\t\t\t\t\t\tthe It is also recognised by professional \n\t\t\t\t\t\tbodies, immigration authorities and other government agencies.</p>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<h4><a class=\"ublink\" href=\"/practice_tests/\" title=\"Free IELTS practice tests\">Free IELTS practice tests</a></h4><br>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<h3>Where can I take IELTS?</h3>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<p>More than <a href=\"/IELTSTestCentreSearch/TestCentreSearch/\" class=\"ulink\">1,100 test centers</a> around the world administer \n\t\t\t\t\t\tIELTS. Some \n\t\t\t\t\t\ttesting centers also offer off-site testing for large groups \n\t\t\t\t\t\tby prior arrangement with IELTS. Students must \n\t\t\t\t\t\tsit all four sub-tests. It consists of four sections, played on cassette \n\t\t\t\t\t\ttape, in order of increasing difficulty. Each section might \n\t\t\t\t\t\tbe a dialogue or a monologue. The test is played once only, \n\t\t\t\t\t\tand the questions for each section must be answered while \n\t\t\t\t\t\tlistening, although time is given for students to check \n\t\t\t\t\t\ttheir answers.</p>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<p>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"/Reading.html\" class=\"ublink\" target=\"_blank\">\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<strong>IELTS Reading test</strong></a> lasts for 60 minutes. \n\t\t\t\t\t\tStudents are given an Academic Reading test, or a General \n\t\t\t\t\t\tTraining Reading test. Both tests consist of three sections, \n\t\t\t\t\t\tand in both tests the sections are in order of increasing \n\t\t\t\t\t\tdifficulty.</p>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<p>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"/Writing.html\" class=\"ublink\" target=\"_blank\">\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<strong>IELTS Writing test</strong></a> also lasts for 60 \n\t\t\t\t\t\tminutes. Again, students take either an Academic Module, \n\t\t\t\t\t\tor a General Training Module. Students must perform two \n\t\t\t\t\t\twriting tasks, which require different styles of writing. \n\t\t\t\t\t\tThere is no choice of question topics.</p>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<p>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"/Speaking.html\" class=\"ublink\" target=\"_blank\">\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<strong>IELTS Speaking test</strong></a> consists of a one-to-one \n\t\t\t\t\t\tinterview with a specially trained examiner.
