WATER</strong> spigot only</li>\n<li>Rinse the reservoir with at least one (1) gallon of clean water</li>\n<li>Drain the water again from the <strong>COLD WATER</strong> spigot only</li>\n<li>Place a teaspoon of unscented household bleach in one (1) gallon of water</li>\n<li>Add this mixture to the reservoir and let stand for a few minutes</li>\n<li>Drain the water from the reservoir</li>\n<li>Place a new bottle of water on your cooler</li>\n<li>Your cooler is now clean!</li>\n<br>\n<p><strong>Other Helpful Tips</strong></p>\n<br>\n<li>The drip tray on your electric
