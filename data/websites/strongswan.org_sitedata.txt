via HTTP or LDAP</span></li>\n<li>Full support of the Online Certificate Status Protocol (OCSP, <a href=\"https://www.ietf.org/rfc/rfc2560.txt\">RFC 2560</a>).</li>\n<li>CA management (OCSP and CRL
