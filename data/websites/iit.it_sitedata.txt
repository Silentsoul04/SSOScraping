research institute</strong>&nbsp;that adopts the organizational&nbsp;<strong>model of a private law foundation</strong>. IIT is overseen by&nbsp;Ministero dell'Istruzione, dell'Università e della Ricerca&nbsp;and&nbsp;Ministero dell'Economia e delle Finanze&nbsp;(the Italian Ministries of Education, Economy and Finance).&nbsp; The Institute was set up according to Italian law 326/2003 with the objective of&nbsp;<strong>promoting excellence</strong>&nbsp;in basic and applied research and<strong>fostering Italy’s economic development</strong>. Construction of the Laboratories started in 2006 and finished in 2009.</p>\n<p>IIT has an&nbsp;<strong>overall staff</strong>&nbsp;of about&nbsp;<strong>1,440 people</strong>. The scientific staff covers about 85% of the total. Out of&nbsp;<strong>45% of researchers coming from abroad</strong>&nbsp;29% are foreigners coming from&nbsp;<strong>more than 50 countries</strong>&nbsp;and 16% are returned Italians. The scientific staff currently consists of approximately&nbsp;<strong>60 Principal Investigators</strong>,&nbsp;<strong>110 researchers and technologists</strong>,&nbsp;<strong>350 post-docs</strong>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<strong>500 PhD students and grant holders</strong>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<strong>130 technicians</strong>. External funding has allowed the creation of more than&nbsp;<strong>330 positions</strong>&nbsp;. The average age is 34 and the gender balance proportion&nbsp; is 41% female against 59% male.</p>\n<p>In 2015 IIT received&nbsp;<strong>96 million&nbsp;</strong>euros in&nbsp;<strong>public funding</strong>&nbsp;(accounting for 80% of its budget) and obtained&nbsp;<strong>22 million euros in external funding</strong>&nbsp;(accounting for 20% of its budget). External funding comes from&nbsp;<strong>18 European Projects</strong>, other&nbsp;<strong>17 national and international competitive projects</strong>&nbsp;and approximately&nbsp;<strong>60 industrial projects</strong>.</p>\n<p>So far IIT accounts for: about&nbsp;<strong>6990 publications</strong>, more than <strong>130 European grants</strong> and <strong>11 ERC grants</strong>, more than&nbsp;<strong>350 patents or patent applications</strong>,&nbsp;<strong>12 up start-ups</strong>&nbsp;and as many&nbsp; which are about to be launched.
