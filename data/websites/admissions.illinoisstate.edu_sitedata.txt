Eat lunch in the student center. Soak in the atmosphere on the Quad. This is your chance to see why Illinois State is a place where you want to live and learn.</p>\n\t\t\t\n                  <h2>Open Houses</h2>\n                  <p>\n                     <a href=\"/visit/open_house/\">Open Houses</a>, held several times throughout the year, are probably the best way for you to learn and see more of Illinois State. Take a campus tour, visit a residence hall, talk with our students, hear about the application and admission process, learn about the majors we offer, talk with various campus representatives, and more.</p>\n                  <h2>Daily Campus Visits</h2>\n                  <p>\n                     <a href=\"/freshman/visit/info_session.php\">Daily Campus Visits</a> are offered most weekdays, Monday through Friday throughout the year, and on scheduled Saturdays from September through April. This two-hour group session includes a presentation by an admissions counselor, a student-led walking tour of campus, and typically a residence hall tour.</p>\n                  <p style=\"margin-left: 30px;\">\n                     <span>\n                        <strong>Saturday Campus Visits&nbsp;</strong>\n                        <br>\n                     </span>\n                     <a href=\"/freshman/visit/saturday.php\">Saturday Campus Visits&nbsp;</a>&nbsp;are offered on select Saturdays from September through April. This two-hour group session includes a presentation by an admissions counselor, a student-led walking tour of campus, and typically a residence hall tour. Saturday Campus Visits start in State Farm Hall of Business.&nbsp;</p>\n                  <p style=\"margin-left: 30px;\">\n                     <strong>Financial Aid and Scholarships Presentations&nbsp;</strong>\n                     <br>\n                     <a href=\"https://forms.illinoisstate.edu/forms/finaid_scholarship_presentation\">Financial Aid and Scholarships Presentations</a>&nbsp;are offered on select&nbsp;Mondays&nbsp;and&nbsp;Fridays during the academic year (October - April). The program is designed to accompany your Daily Campus Visit, providing additional information about costs, financial aid, and scholarships. Space is limited, so please&nbsp;<a href=\"https://forms.illinoisstate.edu/forms/finaid_scholarship_presentation\">register</a>&nbsp;separately if you wish to join us.&nbsp;</p>\n                  <p style=\"margin-left: 30px;\">\n                     <strong>Honors Program Visit&nbsp;&nbsp;</strong>\n                     <br>You can submit a&nbsp;<a href=\"http://honors.illinoisstate.edu/applying/visit/\">visit request</a>&nbsp;to meet individually with an Honors Advisor to learn more about the unique learning opportunities available in the&nbsp;<a href=\"http://honors.illinoisstate.edu/\">Honors Program</a>.&nbsp;Students who have an ACT score in the upper 20s and a GPA of 3.5 or above are encouraged to apply for the&nbsp;Honors Program.&nbsp;</p>\n                  <h2>Admissions Presentations in Spanish</h2>\n                  <p>\n                     <span>This two and a half hour group session includes a presentation in Spanish by an admissions counselor, a student-led walking tour of campus, a residence hall tour, and an optional student panel.&nbsp;Space is limited.&nbsp;<a href=\"/emasforms/CampusInformationSession/Default.aspx?SessType=SPN\">Please make your reservation today</a>!</span>\n                  </p>\n                  <p>\n                     <span>Presentación de Admisión En Español</span>\n                     <br>Esta sesión de dos horas incluye una presentación de un consejero de admisión (en español), un recorrido a pie del campus dirigido por un estudiante&nbsp; y un recorrido de un edificio de residencia. El espacio es limitado. ¡<a href=\"/emasforms/CampusInformationSession/Spanish.aspx\">Por favor haga su reservación hoy</a>!</p>\n                  <h2>Transfer Student Visits</h2>\n                  <p>\n                     <a href=\"/transfer/visit/tis.php\">Transfer Student Visits</a>&nbsp;are designed specifically for students interested in transferring from a two-year community college or a four-year college.&nbsp;</p>\n                  <p>Already admitted? <a href=\"http://universitycollege.illinoisstate.edu/transferdays/\">Attend Transfer Day</a> instead.&nbsp;</p>\n                  <h2>Graduate School Visit</h2>\n                  <p>Contact the <a href=\"http://grad.illinoisstate.edu/\">Graduate School</a> at <a href=\"mailto:gradinfo@ilstu.edu\">gradinfo@ilstu.edu</a> to arrange a campus visit. We will put you in touch with your intended graduate program and schedule a tour with a student in your chosen major or one of Graduate Student Advisory Committee members.</p>\n                  <h2>Group Visits</h2>\n                  <p>\n                     <a href=\"/visit/group/\">Group visits</a> are a great opportunity to introduce your group of high school or community college students to Illinois State University.</p>\n                  <h2>Residence Hall Tours</h2>\n                  <p>Daily Campus Visits and Open Houses typically include a residence hall tour option. You may visit&nbsp;all residence halls during the hours listed below. Additionally, a show room is available during these times in the Community Center of the Cardinal Court&nbsp;student apartment complex, which houses primarily sophomore students.</p>\n                  <p>\n                     <b>Monday-Friday</b>&nbsp;11:30a.m. – 1:30 p.m &amp; 2:30 p.m. – 3:30 p.m.</p>\n                  <p>\n                     <b>Saturday</b>&nbsp;11:30 a.m. - 1:00 p.m.</p>\n                  <p>Our residence halls are secured by electronic access 24-hours a day. Upon arrival, please use the telephone system outside the building entrance to contact the front desk. A tour guide will be happy to show you around the building&nbsp;</p>\n                  <p>Appointments are not necessary Monday through Saturday during these scheduled hours. To make an appointment outside of scheduled hours, please call University Housing Services at (309) 438-8611 between 8:00 a.m. and 4:30 p.m., Monday-Friday. Please allow sufficient time for arrangements to be made. For more information about living on campus, visit&nbsp;<a href=\"http://housing.illinoisstate.edu/\">Housing.IllinoisState.edu</a>.</p>\n                  <h2>City of Chicago Visit</h2>\n                  <p>We're just a short drive or train ride from Chicago, but if you can't make the trip, we'll come to you. Group presentations are available on selected dates at Illinois State's office in downtown Chicago.&nbsp;<a href=\"/emasforms/ChicagoPresentation/Default.aspx\">Schedule your Chicago presentation</a>.</p>\n                  <h2>Meet with an Admissions Representative</h2>\n                  <p>If you are unable to participate in any of the above options, you may&nbsp;<a href=\"/freshman/contact/\">schedule an appointment with an admissions representative</a>.</p>\n                  \n\t                 <div class=\"callout-main\">\n                     <aside class=\"aside\">\n                        <span class=\"aside_top\"><!--Top--></span>\n                        <div class=\"aside_mid\">\n\t\t                         <h2>Lodging in Bloomington-Normal</h2>\n                           <ul>\n                              <li>\n                                 <a href=\"http://www.hotels-rates.com/hotels/locations/Normal/IL/usa/832/#vip&amp;notus=Illinois%2520State%2520University\">Hotels near Illinois State</a>\n                              </li>\n                              <li>\n                                 <a href=\"http://www.visitbn.org/accommodations.cfm\">Bloomington-Normal Area Convention and Visitors Bureau</a>\n                              </li>\n                           </ul>\n                           <h2>Getting to Illinois State</h2>\n                           <ul>\n                              <li>Get&nbsp;<a href=\"/visit/directions.php\">driving directions</a>&nbsp;to campus.</li>\n                              <li>Office of Admissions is located in Hovey Hall, room 201.
