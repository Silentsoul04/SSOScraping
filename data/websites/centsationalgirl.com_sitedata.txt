Yes, I’m spoiled.</p>\n<p>However when I look at it spatially I am irked that the fireplace is in a corner. Yet it was something that had to be reckoned with in this One Room Challenge in this process to makeover my bedroom. Previously there lived an odd mantel made of sheetrock coupled with some not so terrible off-white marble tile. (See below.) The tile wasn’t bad, it was the strange mantel that bugged me.</p>\n<p>My inspiration for my fireplace makeover was a look that was more contemporary, like those tile installations that go floor to ceiling with nothing more. Like <a href=\"//tracking.tapinfluence.com/trk/q0f4h/F8gGk/c?p=UQNxx&amp;i=JxVD2&amp;r=bn4lIrZa8s6oIdkLrQu%2F3E%2B8LrRm5JLOfoLX2cyrTEUjofd1gb5lAjG4xlpBk2Mo\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"nofollow\">this</a> and <a href=\"//tracking.tapinfluence.com/trk/q0f4h/F8gGk/c?p=UQNxx&amp;i=JxVD2&amp;r=bn4lIrZa8s6oIdkLrQu%2F3E%2B8LrRm5JLOfoLX2cyrTEWuRAaA3Qz%2Bl0ULiJZRF1r3\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"nofollow\">this</a> and <a href=\"//tracking.tapinfluence.com/trk/q0f4h/F8gGk/c?p=UQNxx&amp;i=JxVD2&amp;r=6od2TCtag2d3e0%2BBPjGY6LLi72op%2B4t7N9RYn64o6Ch3djdAoP5JELtxxxQ0YH%2FDOdS2XiKdElV04lzAEj3Vyx2dUPjs9aiM\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"nofollow\">this</a> but with a classic marble pattern tile.</p>\n<p style=\"text-align: center;\">This was a DIY team effort.
