           <h1>Registration</h1>\n\n<p>Welcome to Forest Hills School District, a place where learning is a priority, friendliness&nbsp;abounds and students are the heart of the district! FHSD is enriched by its traditions and is highly&nbsp;supported by active and involved parents, community members and partnerships with local&nbsp;universities and businesses.&nbsp;</p>\n\n<h3>PROCESS</h3>\n\n<p>Parents and legal guardians of students register students online<strong>&nbsp;</strong>(see below). Once a&nbsp;parent completes the online registration process, they should come to Central Office (7946 Beechmont Ave.) to provide required registration documents (see below).</p>\n\n<p>Registration hours during the school year are Monday through Friday from 8 a.m.-3:30 p.m. Summer registration hours are Tuesday and Thursdays between 8 a.m.- 3 p.m. No appointment is necessary.</p>\n\n<p>See below for more information on preschool, Head Start and kindergarten registration.</p>\n\n<h3><br>\n<strong>Required Registration Documents</strong></h3>\n\n\n<ul>\n\t<li>parent/guardian driver’s records (if applicable)</li>\n\t<li>ETR/IEP/504 reports/documents&nbsp;(if applicable)</li>\n\t<li>FOR <a href=\"http://www.foresthills.edu/kindergarten.html\">ALL-DAY KINDERGARTEN </a>&nbsp;REGISTRATION ONLY: $75 check for deposit (applied to tuition)</li>\n</ul>\n\n\n\n\n\n<h3>Kindergarten</h3>\n\n<p>Parents can register students for kindergarten in Forest Hills School District beginning Jan. 6. &nbsp;Kindergarten registration is encouraged by March 1.</p>\n\n<p>Once a parent completes the online registration process, they should attend a Kindergarten Round-Up event to provide required documents for registration: parent/guardian driver’s license, child’s current immunization records, child’s official birth certificate, custody papers (if applicable), and proof of residence (mortgage, deed, rental/lease agreement).</p>\n\n<p>Round-Up events will be held Feb. 16 from 4 p.m.-7:30 p.m. and Feb. 17 from 8 a.m.-3 p.m. Parents need to only attend one Round-Up event. &nbsp;Both events will occur at the student’s school of attendance. <a href=\"/content/documents/school-of-attendance-2.pdf\" target=\"_blank\">Find a child’s school of attendance</a></p>\n\n<p>It is preferred that Round-Up events are parent-only events to help expedite the registration process and because students do not need to be present to complete the registration process.</p>\n\n<p>Parents who cannot make the February Round-Up events should complete the online registration and then call Debbie Conover, registrar, at 231-3600 to make an appointment to provide the required documentation.</p>\n\n<p>A $75 deposit (check) is also required for all-day kindergarten registration. &nbsp;This deposit will be applied toward the program tuition.</p>\n\n<p>FHSD recommends that children be five-years-old on or before August 1, 2017 to register for kindergarten. &nbsp;However, for the 2017-18 school year only, students who are five-years-old by Sept. 30, 2017 may also enroll.</p>\n\n<p><a href=\"https://www.foresthills.edu/kindergarten.html\" target=\"_blank\">More information on kindergarten</a><br>\n&nbsp;</p>\n\n\n<h3>PRESCHOOL</h3>\n\n<p>Forest Hills School Parents have one week to complete the registration process and pay a tuition depost of $100. The district will maintain a waiting list&nbsp;for the remaining students who were not able to be placed in the program.</p>\n\n\n\n\n<p>A $100 deposit is due&nbsp;at the time of registration in the form of check or cash.
