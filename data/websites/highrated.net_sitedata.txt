We are neither considered as adults nor as children during our teenage and so expectations of others from us keep confusing us. These are highly embarrassing and cause tension due to their adamancy.</p>\n<p>If you are suffering from teenage related skin problems like acne, remember, you are the only one suffering from the same. 90% of the teenagers around the world suffer from acne and other related skin problems.</p>\n<p><strong>Actually, what is Acne?</strong><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p>\n<p>As mentioned earlier, during teenage hormones in the body undergoes tremendous changes. The Sebaceous Glands which are otherwise in simple terms known as skin’s oil glands secretes an oily fluid named Sebum. This gets formed on the surface of the skin entering through the pores in the skin’s surface. The cells on the surface then mix creating a clog. This clogs increases in number to form pimples.</p>\n<p><strong>Things that aggravate Acne</strong></p>\n<h3>01. The bacterium that spreads across through the fingers creates more acne and pimples.</p>\n<h3>03. Unbalanced hormones in women</h3>\n<p><a href=\"https://www.highrated.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/04.jpg\"><img src=\"https://www.highrated.net/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif\" data-lazy-src=\"https://www.highrated.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/04.jpg\" class=\"aligncenter size-full wp-image-220\" alt=\"\"><noscript>&lt;img class=\"aligncenter size-full wp-image-220\" src=\"https://www.highrated.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/04.jpg\" alt=\"\" /&gt;</noscript></a>The fluctuations in hormone levels in women, particularly during their menstrual cycle causes acne and pimples in women to a large extent</p>\n<h3>05. Due to this fact, the products they give out also tend to be rich in hormones causing fluctuations of the same in humans who consume them.</p>\n<h3>Prevention and cure for Acnes in Teenage</h3>\n<p><a><img class=\"aligncenter size-full wp-image-220\" alt=\"\"></a>Prevention is certainly better than cure, more so with teenage acnes. Cleansing</h3>\n<p><a href=\"https://www.highrated.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/06.jpg\"><img src=\"https://www.highrated.net/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif\" data-lazy-src=\"https://www.highrated.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/06.jpg\" class=\"aligncenter size-full wp-image-220\" alt=\"\"><noscript>&lt;img class=\"aligncenter size-full wp-image-220\" src=\"https://www.highrated.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/06.jpg\" alt=\"\" /&gt;</noscript></a>Keeping your skin clean is the most important aspect to prevent as well as cure the acnes.
