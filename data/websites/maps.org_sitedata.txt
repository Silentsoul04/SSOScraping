class=\"uk-width-medium-1-2\">\n\n<h2><a href=\"/research/mdma\" style=\"color: #fff;\">MDMA-Assisted Psychotherapy</a></h2>\n<p>Our highest priority project is funding clinical trials of 3,4-methylenedioxymethamphetamine (MDMA) as a tool to assist psychotherapy for the treatment of posttraumatic stress disorder (PTSD).</p>\n<p>We are also sponsoring ongoing clinical trials of MDMA-assisted psychotherapy for anxiety associated with life-threatening illness, and MDMA-assisted therapy for social anxiety in autistic adults.</p>\n<p>Preliminary studies have shown that MDMA in conjunction with psychotherapy can help people overcome PTSD, and possibly other disorders as well.</p>\n<!--\n      <div class=\"uk-margin-top\"><a href=\"#\" class=\"uk-icon-button uk-icon-twitter #fff;\">LSD-Assisted Psychotherapy</a></h2>\n\n<p>MAPS has recently completed the first double-blind, placebo-controlled study of the therapeutic use of LSD in human beings since the early 1970s.</p>\n<p>MAPS is interested in LSD for its potential to help people with a variety of conditions, focusing primarily on the treatment of anxiety associated with life-threatening The study results also indicate that LSD-assisted psychotherapy can be safely administered in these subjects, and justify further research.</p>\n\n      </div>\n\n    </div>\n\n  </div>\n</div>\n\t\n\n\n\n<div class=\"uk-container uk-container-center\">\n  <div class=\"uk-grid\" style=\"padding: 25px 0px;\">\n\n    <div class=\"uk-width-medium-1-2\">\n<h2><a href=\"/research/ibogaine-therapy\">Ibogaine Therapy for Drug Addiction</a></h2>\n<p>MAPS-sponsored researchers are collecting observational data for the first prospective ibogaine outcome studies in order to contribute to the growing scientific literature about ibogaine as a treatment for drug addiction.</p>\n<p>Although first-hand accounts indicate that ibogaine is unlikely to be popular as a recreational drug, ibogaine remains classified as a Schedule I drug in the United States (it is also scheduled in Belgium and Switzerland). Yet despite its classification as a drug with a \"high potential for abuse\" and \"no currently accepted medical use,\" people who struggle with substance abuse continue to seek out international clinics or underground providers to receive ibogaine treatment.</p>\n\n    </div>\n    <div class=\"uk-width-medium-1-2\"><a href=\"/research/ibogaine-therapy\"><img src=\"/images/research/home/ibogaine-bottle-website.jpg\" alt=\"ibogaine bottle website\"></a></div>\n  </div>\n\n</div>\n\t\n\n\n\n<div style=\"background: #00264c; padding: 25px 0px; color: #fff;\">\n  <div class=\"uk-container uk-container-center\">\n    <div class=\"uk-grid\">\n<div class=\"uk-width-medium-1-2\"><a href=\"/research/ayahuasca\" style=\"color: #fff;\"><img src=\"/images/research/home/ayahuasca-vine-website.jpg\" alt=\"ayahuasca vine website\"></a></div>\n\n      <div class=\"uk-width-medium-1-2\">\n<h2><a href=\"/research/ayahuasca\" style=\"color: #fff;\">Ayahuasca-Assisted Treatment</a></h2>\n<p>MAPS supports research into the safety and effectiveness of ayahuasca-assisted treatment for drug addiction and PTSD. We also support conferences, meetings, and publications about the scientific, therapeutic, sustainable, and spiritual uses of ayahuasca. We also serve as non-profit fiscal sponsor for organizations that support these uses.</p>\n\n<p>We recently completed the first North American observational study of the safety and long-term effectiveness of ayahuasca treatment for addiction and dependence. The paper describing the results of the study was published in June 2013 in <em style=\"color: #fff;\">Current Drug Abuse Reviews</em>.</p>\n      \n      </div>\n\n\n    </div>\n  </div>\n</div>\n\t\n\n\n\n<div class=\"uk-container uk-container-center\">\n<div class=\"uk-grid\" style=\"padding: 25px 0px;\">\n\n<div class=\"uk-width-medium-1-2\">\n<h2><a href=\"/research/mmj\">Medical Marijuana</a></h2>\n<p>MAPS has been working since 1992 to sponsor  FDA-approved clinical studies into the safety and efficacy of botanical marijuana as a prescription medicine for specific medical uses. We've obtained approval from FDA and from Institutional Review Boards for several different protocols but were unable to obtain approval to purchase federally-legal marijuana from the National Institute on Drug Abuse (NIDA).</p>\n<p>On March 14, 2014, the U.S. Public Health Service approved our request to purchase NIDA marijuana for our study of smoked whole plant (botanical) marijuana for symptoms of PTSD in U.S. veterans, an historic shift in federal policy. On December 17, 2014, MAPS was awarded a $2.15 million grant from the state of Colorado to conduct the study. The study has received full approval from the FDA, DEA, and Institutional Review Boards (IRBs). We anticipate starting the study in the summer of 2016 at Johns Hopkins University and in Phoenix, Ariz.</p>\n\n</div>\n<div class=\"uk-width-medium-1-2\"><a href=\"/research/mmj\"><img src=\"/images/research/home/marijuana-website.jpg\" alt=\"marijuana website\"></a></div>\n</div>\n</div>\n\n</div></div>\n</section>\n\n                                </div>\n                \n            </div>\n        \n                    <div class=\"tm-block tm-block-default\">\n\n                                <div class=\"uk-container uk-container-center\">\n                \n                <section class=\"tm-bottom-c uk-grid\" data-uk-grid-match=\"{target:'> div > .uk-panel'}\" data-uk-grid-margin=\"\">\n<div class=\"uk-width-1-1\"><div class=\"\">\n\t<h2 style=\"text-align: center; border-bottom: 1px solid #ddd; width: 100%;\">Our Values</h2>\n\n<div class=\"uk-grid\">\n<div class=\"uk-width-large-1-4 uk-width-medium-1-2\" style=\"margin-bottom: 20px;\">\n<h4>Transparency</h4>\n<p>Information is shared openly and clearly.
