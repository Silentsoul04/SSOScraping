directions</h3></header><div class=\"blurb\"><div class=\"box\"><p>Always wanted to travel across US but were not sure if you have enough time for that? Did you ever try to figure out <a href=\"https://help.furkot.com/features/distance-and-travel-time.html\" rel=\"noopener\" target=\"_blank\">how long it takes</a> to drive from Yosemite to Yellowstone or from Lisbon to Rome?</p><p>Start clicking on the map. <a href=\"https://help.furkot.com/how-to/new-trip-wizard.html\" rel=\"noopener\" target=\"_blank\">Choose your destination</a> and have Furkot calculate how much time you need to get there. Set daily limits on the travel time so none of your sightseeing has to be done under moonlight — unless, of course, that's what you want.</p><p>Don't worry about putting stops in order. Furkot attempts to plan a route for your trip in the most efficient way. If you don't want efficient, you can <a href=\"//help.furkot.com/how-to/reorder-stops.html\" rel=\"noopener\" target=\"_blank\">drag your stops</a> around or even <a href=\"https://help.furkot.com/how-to/reverse.html\" rel=\"noopener\" target=\"_blank\">reverse your itinerary</a>.</p><p>Tell us how long you want to drive every day, and we will show you where to <a href=\"https://help.furkot.com/features/automatic-overnight-stops.html\" rel=\"noopener\" target=\"_blank\">stop for the night</a> Furkot suggests overnight stops along the route based on your schedule and preferences.</p><p>Furkot can help you with all your travel plans: a day trip, a weekend getaway, or a continent-crossing road trip. And <a href=\"https://help.furkot.com/features/travel-mode.html\" rel=\"noopener\" target=\"_blank\">driving is not the only option</a>. You can also schedule motorcycle rides, biking excursions and walking tours with Furkot.</p><p><a href=\"//help.furkot.com/getting-started/planning-a-trip.html\" target=\"_blank\">Read more&nbsp;…</a></p></div></div><div class=\"screenshot\"><div class=\"box\"><picture><source srcset=\"https://static.furkot.com/img/welcome/9uxud1lzGk-trip.webp\" type=\"image/webp\"><img src=\"https://static.furkot.com/img/welcome/CGzAvz9y8g-trip.png\" alt=\"Trip map\" crossorigin=\"anonymous\"></picture></div></div></section><section class=\"places\"><header><h3>It's not just about driving</h3></header><div class=\"blurb\"><div class=\"box\"><p>It's also about all the things you do on the way. Leave your car parked for a couple of hours and hike one of the trails from the vast <a href=\"http://www.everytrail.com\" rel=\"noopener\" target=\"_blank\">Everytrail</a> collection.</p><p>Need some hints? Make sure to check interesting places described on <a href=\"https://www.wikipedia.org/\" rel=\"noopener\" target=\"_blank\">Wikipedia</a>. They are one click away from ending up on your road trip stop list.</p><p>If your car resembles ours you can't drive on water, but Furkot will route you to the closest dry land point for those spots that do require a boat ride. You can <a href=\"https://help.furkot.com/features/dive-sites.html\" rel=\"noopener\" target=\"_blank\">drive and dive</a> with Furkot.</p><p>Are you after fresh powder? Or maybe shredding corduroy is your thing? Trees or bowls? Skis or snowboard? Regardless of the answer, you can use Furkot to find ski resorts — courtesy of <a href=\"http://www.epicski.com\" rel=\"noopener\" target=\"_blank\">EpicSki</a>.</p><p>You can even plan meal stops and add gas stations to your itinerary. Not to mention biking paths, museums, monuments, <a href=\"https://help.furkot.com/features/points-of-interest.html#outdoor-sports\" rel=\"noopener\" target=\"_blank\">climbing routes</a>, national parks, scenic views — add as many as you want. Furkot will keep you on track. You just need to show up.</p><p><a href=\"https://help.furkot.com/getting-started/attractions.html\" target=\"_blank\">Read more&nbsp;…</a></p></div></div><div class=\"screenshot\"><div class=\"box\"><picture><source srcset=\"https://static.furkot.com/img/welcome/kQF1YQMn6Q-attractions.webp\" type=\"image/webp\"><img src=\"https://static.furkot.com/img/welcome/PFg8TkStSA-attractions.png\" alt=\"Attractions search\" crossorigin=\"anonymous\"></picture></div></div></section><section class=\"lodging\"><header><h3>Campgrounds? Whenever available, Furkot displays exactly <a href=\"https://help.furkot.com/features/current-hotel-prices.html\" rel=\"noopener\" target=\"_blank\">what you are going to pay</a> (including taxes and service fees). You are in charge of what they charge.</p><p>When you are ready to book, Furkot will take you directly to your favorite booking site and pre-fill the check-in and check-out dates so that you don't have to worry about reserving that hard found lodging on the wrong night.</p><p>Booking <a href=\"https://help.furkot.com/how-to/book-trip.html\" rel=\"noopener\" target=\"_blank\">all hotels</a> for your trip can be a daunting experience: multiple cities, multiple booking sites and many dates and details to mistype?
