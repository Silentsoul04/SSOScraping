Trust me, I still cared about it a lot, and I still care now, but I did lose&nbsp;a lot of motivation. Around this time, I also started up&nbsp;the <a href=\"https://twitter.com/Poptropica_Tips\">PTFP’s Twitter</a> and my own <a href=\"http://sterlingwolfy.deviantart.com/\">DeviantArt</a>.</span></p>\n<p><span style=\"font-weight:400;\">One of the things I’d like to point out in this section is that I owe a lot of my blogging career to other people.
