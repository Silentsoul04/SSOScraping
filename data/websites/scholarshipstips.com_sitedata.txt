Students, University of the West of England, UK</a></h2> </header><div class=\"entry-content\"><p>Full tuition fee scholarships are available for all <em>international / overseas (non UK &amp; EU) students</em> enrolling postgraduate degree at <strong>University of the West of England – Bristol, UK</strong>.</p><p>There is no restriction on the nationality of the students, except that the students must come from outside EU countries and students must not have previously studied in the UK. However, applicants must meet all the entry requirements for the postgraduate degree program and must meet the English language (IELTS) admission requirement. Students must also have been offered a conditional / unconditional place from the University of the West of England, Bristol, UK.</p><h3>Level of Education</h3><p>Scholarships available for postgraduate degree only.</p><h3>Subjects or Fields of Study</h3><p>Scholarships available for all subjects, <strong>except</strong>:</p><ul><li>Master of Business Administration (MBA)</li><li>Bar Professional Training Course (BPTC)</li><li>Legal Practice Course (LPC)</li><li>Graduate Diploma in Law (GDL)</li><li>Master of Science (MSc) Robotics</li><li>PhD</li><li>Professional development courses</li></ul><h3>Amount of Scholarships</h3><p>This scholarship will pay full 100% tuition fees.</p><h3>Duration of Scholarships</h3><p>Scholarships available for 1 (one) academic year.</p><p> <a
