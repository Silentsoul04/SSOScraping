2017</time></span><span><a href=\"https://www.globaltranz.com/category/blog-post/\" title=\"View all posts in: “Blog Post”\"><i class=\"x-icon-bookmark\" data-x-icon=\"\"></i> Blog Post</a>, <a class=\"entry-content content\"><p>Recruiting strong people is critical to company growth. In a signing ceremony with the U.S. Army on March 30, 2017, GlobalTranz officially joined the Army PaYS program — a channel for recruiting highly trained logistics and supply chain management professionals who have also served our country.</p><p><span id=\"ember2503\" class=\"ember-view\">GlobalTranz was pleased to host U.S. Army LTC David S. Clukey, Phoenix Recruiting Battalion Commander, CPT Matthew T. Peterson and the soldiers of the Phoenix Recruiting Battalion to formalize the partnership.<br> </span></p><p>Here are a few snapshots from the ceremony, led by GlobalTranz chairman and CEO, Bob Farrell, and U.S. Army Lieutenant Colonel, David Clukey.</p><p><img class=\"alignnone wp-image-4297 size-large\" src=\"https://www.globaltranz.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/GTZ-and-Army-PaYS-signing-Bob-Farrell-speech-1024x680.jpg\" alt=\"\" width=\"714\" height=\"474\"></p><p><img class=\"alignnone wp-image-4298 size-large\" src=\"https://www.globaltranz.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/GTZ-and-Army-PaYS-signing-ceremony-Army-speech-1024x741.jpg\" alt=\"\" width=\"714\" height=\"517\"></p><p><img class=\"alignnone wp-image-4299 size-large\" src=\"https://www.globaltranz.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/GTZ-and-Army-PaYS-signing-ceremony-partnership-official-1024x731.jpg\" alt=\"\" width=\"714\" height=\"510\"></p><p><img class=\"alignnone wp-image-4300 size-large\" src=\"https://www.globaltranz.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/GTZ-and-Army-PaYS-signing-ceremony-plaque-1024x683.jpg\" alt=\"\" width=\"714\" height=\"476\"></p><p><span id=\"ember2503\" class=\"ember-view\">The ceremony included a wonderful rendition of the national anthem by GlobalTranz’ own Victoria Faris. The U.S. Army PaYS Program is a strategic partnership between the Army and a cross section of private industry, academia and state and local public institutions. The U.S. Army PaYS Program guarantees soldiers a job interview and possible employment after their service in the Army. The PaYS Program provides America’s youth with the unique opportunity to simultaneously serve their country and prepare for their future.</span></p><div class=\"sharedaddy sd-sharing-enabled\"><div
