Aus den Ergebnissen werden im letzten Teil einige Schlussfolgerungen für Politik und Zivilgesellschaft abgeleitet.</p>\n\t</div>\n\t\n\t\t\n\t\t\t<a href=\"/component/content/article/271-general/52938-neues-arbeitspapier-globale-partnerschaften.html\" title=\"Neues Arbeitspapier: Globale Partnerschaften\" class=\"readon\">\n\t\t\t\tRead more...\t\t</a>\n\t\t\n\t\n</div>\n\n\n<span class=\"article_separator\">&nbsp;</span> \n\n<div class=\"contentpaneopen\">\n\n\t\t<div class=\"article-title clearfix\">\n\t\t<h2 class=\"contentheading\">\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"/component/content/article/271-general/52937-closing-the-gender-gap.html\" class=\"contentpagetitle\">Closing the Gender Gap</a>\n\t\t\t\t\t</h2>\n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\n\t\t\n\t\t\n\t\t\n\t<div class=\"article-content\">\n\t\t\t\t<img src=\"/images/images/adamsjudd_cswwee_en_web20.jpg\" alt=\"adamsjudd_cswwee_en_web20\" style=\"margin: 5px; border-color: #000000; border-width: 1px; border-style: solid; float: left;\" height=\"164\" width=\"127\">Almost 22 years have passed since the Fourth World Conference on Women was held in Beijing, marking a turning point for women’s rights activists around the world. The struggle to empower women and to combat gender inequality goes hand in hand with the struggle for women’s human rights. The increasing application of human rights instruments from local to global continues to be the hallmark of organizing that crosses sectors, policy tracks and borders. The work of human rights advocates and defenders has required establishing new rules and systems as well as removing discrimination and bias in the application of existing ones. This is as relevant across territorial borders as within them and the gap between transnational economic activities and global economic governance can magnify inequalities or nullify measures to overcome them.</p>\n\t</div>\n\t\n\t\t\n\t\t\t<a href=\"/component/content/article/271-general/52935-women-rights-without-borders.html\" title=\"Women rights without borders\" class=\"readon\">\n\t\t\t\tRead more...\t\t</a>\n\t\t\n\t\n</div>\n\n\n<span class=\"article_separator\">&nbsp;</span> \n\n<div class=\"contentpaneopen\">\n\n\t\t<div class=\"article-title clearfix\">\n\t\t<h2 class=\"contentheading\">\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"/component/content/article/271-general/52933-peasants-negotiating-a-global-policy-space.html\" class=\"contentpagetitle\">Peasants Negotiating a Global Policy Space</a>\n\t\t\t\t\t</h2>\n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\n\t\t\n\t\t\n\t\t\n\t<div class=\"article-content\">\n\t\t\t\t<p><img src=\"/images/images/9781138214873.jpg\" alt=\"9781138214873\" style=\"margin: 4px; border-color: #000000; border-width: 1px; border-style: solid; float: left;\" width=\"127\" height=\"192\">Being the public voice of over 180 member organisations across nearly 90 countries, La Vía Campesina, the global peasant movement, has planted itself firmly on the international scene. Since the reform of the CFS in 2009, civil society actors engage in the policy processes of this UN Committee from a self-designed and autonomous global Civil Society Mechanism. The author sheds light on the strategies, tensions, debates, and reconfigurations arising from rural actors moving between every day struggles in the fields and those of the UN arena.</p>\n\t</div>\n\t\n\t\t\n\t\t\t<a href=\"/component/content/article/271-general/52933-peasants-negotiating-a-global-policy-space.html\" title=\"Peasants Negotiating a Global Policy Space\" class=\"readon\">\n\t\t\t\tRead more...\t\t</a>\n\t\t\n\t\n</div>\n\n\n<span class=\"article_separator\">&nbsp;</span> \n\n<div class=\"contentpaneopen\">\n\n\t\t<div class=\"article-title clearfix\">\n\t\t<h2 class=\"contentheading\">\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<a href=\"/component/content/article/271-general/52932-2017-02-09-13-25-57.html\" class=\"contentpagetitle\">Neuerscheinung: SDGs für die Bundesländer</a>\n\t\t\t\t\t</h2>\n\t\t\t</div>\n\t\t\n\t\t\n\t\t\n\t\t\n\t<div class=\"article-content\">\n\t\t\t\t<p><img src=\"/images/images/GPF-Briefing_0117_SDGs_Bundeslander.jpg\" alt=\"GPF-Briefing_0117_SDGs_Bundeslander\" style=\"margin: 4px; border-color: #000000; border-width: 1px; border-style: solid; float: left;\" width=\"127\" height=\"180\">Die Umsetzung der 2030-Agenda und der in ihr enthaltenen Ziele für nachhaltige Entwicklung (Sustainable Development Goals, SDGs) ist nicht allein Aufgabe der Bundesregierung. Bereits seit der Jahrtausendwende gab es einen regelrechten Partnerschaftsboom. Diverse globale Partnerschaften und Multi-Akteurs-Initiativen, u.a. in den Bereichen Gesundheit, Ernährung und Energie, wurden seitdem ins Leben gerufen. Dem Boom liegt die Annahme zugrunde, Regierungen seien allein weder strukturell noch finanziell in der Lage, die globalen Probleme zu bewältigen. Kooperationen, die Wirtschaft und Zivilgesellschaft einschließen, gelten dagegen als pragmatisch, lösungsorientiert und effektiv. Für die Umsetzung der SDGs gelten sie bei Vielen als unentbehrlich.
