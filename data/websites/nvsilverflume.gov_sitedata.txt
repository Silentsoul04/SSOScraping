business. SilverFlume eliminates about 80% of redundant steps by collecting and streamlining business information to partner agencies. SilverFlume simplifies compliance, enabling you to focus on growing your business.</p>\n<h4>What's in a Name?</h4>\n<p style=\"font-size: 14.444445px;\">When SilverFlume launched in 2012, the Nevada Secretary of State wanted to distinguish Nevada's business portal from the vast array of other \"portals\" on the internet. Seeking a unique term with Nevada roots, the Secretary branded the state's business portal \"SilverFlume\".&nbsp;<span style=\"font-size: 14.0px;\">Flumes are artificial water channels and were used extensively to carry minerals and timber in a quick and efficient manner in the mines that made Nevada “The Silver State\". SilverFlume replicates the fluidity of those waterways by acting as a portal to transfer information.</span><span style=\"font-size: 14.0px;\">&nbsp;The internet has many \"portals,\" but there is only one \"SilverFlume\".</span></p>\n<p><span>SilverFlume is more than a great name. It is a valuable tool to start and expand businesses that will grow Nevada’s economy… together, in one place.</span></p>\n<h4>Are you ready to get started?</h4>\n<p>The&nbsp;<a title=\"New Business Checklist\" href=\"https://nvsilverflume.gov/checklist\">New Business Checklist</a> can provide you a quick summary of which licenses you'll need.</p>\n\t\t</div>\n\n\t\t<div id=\"features\"
