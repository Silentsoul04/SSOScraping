class=\"sectionedit2\" id=\"css-working-group-wiki\">CSS Working Group Wiki</h1>\n<div class=\"level1\">\n\n<p>\nThe <abbr title=\"Cascading Style Sheets\">CSS</abbr> Working Group is the <a href=\"http://www.w3.org/\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"http://www.w3.org/\" rel=\"nofollow\">W3C</a> working group chartered to develop <a href=\"http://www.w3.org/Style/CSS/\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"http://www.w3.org/Style/CSS/\" rel=\"nofollow\">Cascading Style Sheets</a> (<abbr title=\"Cascading Style Sheets\">CSS</abbr>). We are responsible both for the <abbr title=\"Cascading Style Sheets\">CSS</abbr> specifications and for their <a href=\"http://www.w3.org/Style/CSS/Test/\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"http://www.w3.org/Style/CSS/Test/\" rel=\"nofollow\">conformance test suites</a>. Our <a href=\"http://www.w3.org/Style/CSS/current-work\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"http://www.w3.org/Style/CSS/current-work\" rel=\"nofollow\">roadmap</a> is maintained on the <abbr title=\"World Wide Web Consortium\">W3C</abbr> website.\n</p>\n\n<p>\nThis wiki is to help the CSSWG record <a href=\"/ideas\" class=\"wikilink1\" title=\"ideas\">resolutions</a> and open <a href=\"/spec\" class=\"wikilink1\" title=\"spec\">issues</a>, to share them with public, and to encourage public involvement in the development of <abbr title=\"Cascading Style Sheets\">CSS</abbr> and its <a href=\"/test\" class=\"wikilink1\" title=\"test\">test suites</a>. Contributions to this wiki are governed by the same conditions as the <a href=\"http://www.w3.org/Consortium/Legal/2006/07-incoming-wiki-copyright.html\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"http://www.w3.org/Consortium/Legal/2006/07-incoming-wiki-copyright.html\" rel=\"nofollow\">W3C Mobile Web Wiki Contribution Policy</a>. Note that editing is restricted to <abbr title=\"Cascading Style Sheets\">CSS</abbr> Working Group members except in the Testing section (which is open to all).\n</p>\n\n<p>\nThe <abbr title=\"Cascading Style Sheets\">CSS</abbr> Working Group welcomes your feedback on our work! Public discussion of our specs happens in github issues on the in <a href=\"https://github.com/w3c/csswg-drafts/\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"https://github.com/w3c/csswg-drafts/\" rel=\"nofollow\">csswg-drafts</a>, <a href=\"https://github.com/w3c/fxtf-drafts/\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"https://github.com/w3c/fxtf-drafts/\" rel=\"nofollow\">fxtf-drafts</a>, and <a href=\"https://github.com/w3c/css-houdini-drafts/\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"https://github.com/w3c/css-houdini-drafts/\" rel=\"nofollow\">css-houdini-drafts</a> repositories (corresponding to <a href=\"https://drafts.csswg.org/\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"https://drafts.csswg.org/\" rel=\"nofollow\">CSS drafts</a>, <a href=\"https://drafts.fxtf.org/\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"https://drafts.fxtf.org/\" rel=\"nofollow\">FXTF drafts</a>, and <a href=\"https://drafts.css-houdini.org/\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"https://drafts.css-houdini.org/\" rel=\"nofollow\">Houdini drafts</a>) and on the archived <a href=\"http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/www-style/\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/www-style/\" rel=\"nofollow\">www-style</a> mailing list, while discussion of our test suites happens on <a href=\"http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/public-css-testsuite/\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/public-css-testsuite/\" rel=\"nofollow\">public-css-testsuite</a>. If you are part of a <abbr title=\"World Wide Web Consortium\">W3C</abbr> Member organization, you can also access our <a href=\"http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Member/w3c-css-wg/\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Member/w3c-css-wg/\" rel=\"nofollow\">internal mailing list</a>. Minutes of our meetings and publishing updates are posted to the <a href=\"http://www.w3.org/blog/CSS/\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"http://www.w3.org/blog/CSS/\" rel=\"nofollow\">CSS WG Blog</a>. See list of <a href=\"/communications\" class=\"wikilink1\" title=\"communications\">communications</a> channels.\n</p>\n\n<p>\nThe working group uses the <a href=\"/planning\" class=\"wikilink1\" title=\"planning\">planning</a> page to coordinate topics for our face-to-face meetings.\n</p>\n\n<p>\n</p><p></p><div class=\"noteclassic\">\nDue to abuse by spam bots, account registration has been disabled on this wiki. Registration is available on <a href=\"https://test.csswg.org/shepherd/register/\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"https://test.csswg.org/shepherd/register/\" rel=\"nofollow\">Shepherd</a> and accounts are shared with the wiki.\n\n</div><p></p>\n<p></p>\n\n<p>\nSee <a href=\"http://wiki.csswg.org/?do=recent\" class=\"urlextern\" title=\"http://wiki.csswg.org/?do=recent\" rel=\"nofollow\">recent
