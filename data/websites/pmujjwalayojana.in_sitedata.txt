The basic details such as Name, Contact details, Jan Dhan / Bank account number, Aadhar card number etc are required to fill in the application form. The applicants also need to mention their requirement of cylinder type i.e. 14.2KG or 5KG.</p>\n<p><a href=\"http://www.pmujjwalayojana.in/pradhan-mantri-ujjwala-yojana-application-forms/\">KYC Application forms for Ujjwala Yojana</a> can also be downloaded online and submitted to nearest LPG outlet along with required documents.</p>\n<h3>Eligibility for PM Ujjwala Yojana</h3>\n<p>The identification of eligible BPL families will be made on the basis of SECC-2011 data. However below is the basic eligibility criteria for the scheme.</p>\n<ul>\n<li>The applicant’s name should be in the list of SECC-2011 data.</li>\n<li>The applicant should be a women above the age of 18 years.</li>\n<li>The women applicant should belong to BPL (Below Poverty Line).</li>\n<li>The women applicant should have a saving bank account in any nationalized bank across the country.</li>\n<li>The applicant’s household should not already own a LPG connection in anyone’s name.</li>\n</ul>\n<p>The detailed eligibility criteria of the Ujjwala Yojana is available <a href=\"http://www.pmujjwalayojana.in/pradhan-mantri-ujjwala-yojana-eligibility-criteria/\">here</a>.</p>\n<p><a href=\"http://www.pmujjwalayojana.in/state-wise-ujjwala-yojana-list-bpl-households-secc-2011-data/\">Ujjwala Yojana List</a> of BPL candidates can be verified by checking the name in the <a href=\"http://www.sarkariyojna.co.in/download-bpl-list-pm-ujjwala-yojana-secc-2011-data/\" target=\"_blank\">SECC-2011 data</a>.</p>\n<h3>List of Required Documents for Ujjwala Yojana Application</h3>\n<p>Below is the list of mandatory documents to be attached along with the filled application form.</p>\n<ol>\n<li>BPL Certificate Authorized by Panchayat Pradhan / Municipality Chairman</li>\n<li>BPL Ration Card</li>\n<li>One Photo ID (Aadhar Card or Voter ID Card)</li>\n<li>One Recent Passport Size Photograph</li>\n</ol>\n<p>View the <a href=\"http://www.pmujjwalayojana.in/documents-required-pradhan-mantri-ujjwala-yojana/\">complete list of documents for Ujjwala Yojana application</a> which can be attached on the basis of requirement.</p>\n<h3>Budget and Funding of Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana</h3>\n<p>The government has already allocated Rs. 2000 crore for the Ujjwala Yojana implementation for the financial year 2016-17. Government will distribute LPG connections to about 1.5 Crore BPL families within the current financial year.</p>\n<p>A total budgetary allocation of Rs. 8000 Crore has been made by the government for the implementation of the scheme over three years. The scheme will be implemented using the money saved in LPG subsidy through the “Give-it-Up” campaign.</p>\n<p><strong>Financial Assistance</strong><br>\nThe scheme provides a financial support of Rs. 1600 for each LPG connection to the eligible BPL households. The connections under the scheme will be given in the name of women head of the households.</p>\n<p>The government will also provide EMI facility for meeting the cost of stove and refill.</p>\n<h3>Implementation of PM Ujjwala Yojana</h3>\n<p>The scheme will be implemented by the Ministry of Petroleum &amp; Natural Gas. This is first time in the history that Ministry of Petroleum &amp; Natural Gas is implementing such an enormous welfare scheme which will benefit Crores of women belonging to the poorest households.</p>\n<p>This Scheme would be implemented over three years, namely, the FY 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19.</p>\n<h4>Current state of LPG distribution in India</h4>\n<p>India is home to more than 24 Crore households out of which about 10 Crore households are still deprived of LPG as cooking fuel and have to rely on firewood, coal, dung – cakes etc. as primary source of
