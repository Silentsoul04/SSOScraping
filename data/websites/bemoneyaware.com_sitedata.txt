How will GST impact Stock Market? The introduction of the landmark goods and services tax (GST) on July 1st heralds a new era in India’s economic growth. However, the run-up to the final passage of the bill has been plagued with speculation, leaving corporate India to brace itself for the immediate impact across sectors and markets.
